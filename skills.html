I am first a husband and father of two (soon to be three) awesome boys.
I remember being at a STEM event with my son during his first year of school.
I noticed how comfortable he and the other children were with the devices and technology they were using.
I also noticed how engaging the process was for both them using the tech and for me simply witnessing it.
I realized that I had missed that excitement and wonder that I was feeling in that moment.
This is something I can teach myself.
This is something that I can teach my children.
I then thought back on my almost year of HTML / CSS work that I had started before he was born.
That JavaScript was the most confusing thing I had ever laid eyes on... It would take a lot of work, but I would do anything for my family.
I decided at that moment that I would finish what I had started and pursue a career in technology.
